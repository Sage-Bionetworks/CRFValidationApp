About Stair Step test

Why this test

Recording your heart rate recovery after going up and down a stair step for 3 minutes has been used to assess ones cardiovascular fitness level.

What to do

  1. Find a stair step that you are comfortable going up and down for 3 minutes (can be inside or outside).
  2. Take a picture of the step that you will being going up and down.
  3. Measure your heart rate within this app before running for 12 minutes.
  4. Step up and then down a single step at a speed of 24 steps per minute for 3 minutes.
  5. Measure your heart rate immediately after the 3 minute stair step session.
